# Dino Tails — World Bible

## World Overview
The Dino Tails world is a prehistoric jungle land called Fernwood Valley.
It is sunny, colorful, and full of giant plants, sparkling rivers,
volcanoes that occasionally hiccup, and friendly creatures of all kinds.
The humor is warm, silly, and slapstick. Nothing is ever truly dangerous.
Everyone means well even when things go hilariously wrong.

## Main Characters

### Rexi
- Species: Baby T-Rex
- Appearance: Small round body, bright green scales, tiny arms that
  cannot reach anything, huge expressive eyes, wide toothy grin,
  stumpy tail that knocks things over
- Personality: Wildly overconfident, endlessly optimistic, always
  convinced the next plan will work perfectly, genuinely shocked
  when it does not
- Special skill: Coming up with terrible ideas that somehow accidentally
  work out in the end
- Weakness: Tiny arms make everything harder than it needs to be
- Catchphrase: "This is definitely going to work."
- Running gag: Always knocks something over with tail while celebrating
- SD prompt block: "baby T-Rex dinosaur, tiny arms, big expressive eyes, bright green scales, wide toothy grin, round body, stumpy tail"

### Lonnie
- Species: Brontosaurus
- Appearance: Pale yellow, very long neck, small head, gentle eyes,
  always has a leaf stuck somewhere on body
- Personality: Calm, patient, slightly worried about everything,
  the voice of reason nobody listens to
- Special skill: Can reach things very high up, surprisingly good at baking
- Weakness: Gets dizzy when looking down from height
- Running gag: Always says "I have a bad feeling about this" right
  before something explodes
- SD prompt block: "pale yellow Brontosaurus, very long neck, small gentle head, leaf stuck on body, worried expression"

### Pterry
- Species: Pterodactyl
- Appearance: Bright orange wings, tiny body, very large beak,
  goggles always pushed up on forehead
- Personality: Dramatic, loves an audience, convinced every moment
  is the most important moment in history
- Special skill: Flying, delivering dramatic speeches, spotting things
  from the air
- Weakness: Cannot swim, terrified of fish
- Running gag: Narrates everything out loud as a nature documentary
- SD prompt block: "bright orange Pterodactyl, tiny body, large beak, goggles on forehead, dramatic expression, wings spread"

### Spike
- Species: Stegosaurus
- Appearance: Deep purple, diamond shaped plates on back that glow
  faintly when excited, round belly, small eyes
- Personality: Very enthusiastic about everything, extremely loud,
  has no volume control whatsoever
- Special skill: Plates glow different colors based on emotion
- Weakness: Accidentally breaks things by sitting on them
- Running gag: Whispers something that is still deafeningly loud
- SD prompt block: "deep purple Stegosaurus, glowing diamond plates on back, round belly, small excited eyes, enthusiastic pose"

### Mara
- Species: Triceratops
- Appearance: Sky blue with three golden horns, always carrying
  a satchel full of snacks and supplies
- Personality: Extremely prepared, has a solution for everything,
  slightly smug about it
- Special skill: Has exactly the right item for any situation
- Weakness: Cannot improvise, falls apart when plans change
- Running gag: Pulls increasingly improbable items from her satchel
- SD prompt block: "sky blue Triceratops, three golden horns, brown satchel bag, smug smile, organized and prepared"

## Locations in Fernwood Valley
- The Big Fern Forest — dense jungle, things fall on characters
- Bubbling Mud Flats — great for splashing, terrible for staying clean
- Mount Hiccup — friendly volcano that hiccups, sends rocks flying
- The Sparkling River — crystal clear, home to opinionated fish
- Lonnie's Kitchen Tree — giant tree where Lonnie bakes
- The Echo Caves — everything echoes back louder and slightly wrong
- Sky Meadow — floating island only Pterry can reach easily
- The Ancient Stones — mysterious rocks with carvings nobody can read

## World Rules
- Nothing is ever truly dangerous — worst case is embarrassing or messy
- Characters always make up after arguments by the end of the book
- Every problem is solved through teamwork even if accidentally
- Adults do not exist — the characters handle everything themselves
- Magic does not exist but coincidences happen constantly

## Humor Style
- Slapstick physical comedy
- Dramatic overreaction to small problems
- Characters completely misunderstanding each other
- Plans that fail in the most spectacular way possible
- Running gags that pay off at the end
- Parent-friendly jokes layered inside the kids humor

## Series Tone
Warm, funny, never mean-spirited. Characters embarrass themselves but
are never humiliated. Friends tease each other but always with love.
Every book ends with everyone happy, fed, and slightly muddy.
